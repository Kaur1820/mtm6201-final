Become a Volunteer

Change a dog’s life — and your own — by volunteering with us.

Why Volunteer?

Volunteers help walk dogs, run events, foster pets, provide transport, and support our rescue efforts.

Volunteer Roles

Dog Walking

Provide exercise and enrichment for our rescue dogs.

Event Helper

Assist with adoption events, fundraisers, and awareness activities.

Foster Care

Provide temporary shelter and love until dogs are adopted.
